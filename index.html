Welcome to Stardew Valley Zodiac!

In this game, you’ll be able to find out which character’s birthday you match with.

  1. Enter your birthday in the [MM/DD/YY] text box.
  2. Press on the [Find Your Character] button.

To pause or play the music, press on the play/pause button in the top right corner.
